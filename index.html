FAQ

  • Is Tower Hero – Tower Defense free to play?

    Yes, the game is free with optional in-app purchases for enhancements.

  • Do I need to be online to play?

    No. The game supports offline play, though some features may require internet.

  • How do I unlock new heroes and towers?

    You can unlock them by progressing through levels, completing achievements, or earning in-game rewards.

    Yes. Replaying levels is encouraged to earn more stars and improve your tactics.

    The game is available on Android, with iOS support under development.
